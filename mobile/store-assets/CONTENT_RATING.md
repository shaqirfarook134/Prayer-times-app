- Primary users are adults who pray
- Still allows teenagers to use the app

- [x] **All ages (including children)**

**BUT this requires:**
- Additional privacy compliance
- More strict data collection rules
- Privacy policy must address children's data

**Recommendation: Select "Ages 13 and up"** to keep things simple.
